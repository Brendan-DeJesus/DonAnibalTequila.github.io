Don Anibal

Tequila Extra Añejo

Created with the finest agaves harvested from the highlands of Jalisco, then aged for 8 years in American oak barrels. The region and aging process lend to this tequila’s diversity of aromas and exquisite blend of flavors. This tequila’s appealing, deep amber appearance is complemented by its oaky, sweet aroma with hints of vanilla bean, caramel, maple syrup and toasted walnut. This tequila is extremely smooth with pleasantly lingering flavors of vanilla, chocolate, coffee and dried fruits. Pairs well with steak, dark chocolate, a cigar…or by itself. Bottle personalization available.

Tequila Silver

Ranked as one of the best tasting tequilas in the world. The delicate floral aroma of this tequila is balanced with flavor undertones of citrus, caramel and vanilla, making this tequila a smooth sipping experience. This tequila will not overpower a cocktail, when mixed with another beverage. With no additional sugars, this tequila may be enjoyed without effects caused by added sugar.
